# UoG_TDP-EE3_Team_7
Design Files for the 3rd year EEE team design project at the University of Glasgow. Line following rover, with obstacle avoidance, traffic lights detection, remote control using an ESP-32 connected to a laptop and a Logitech 3D Pro Joystick.
The  Centeral processor is a Freescale NXP Frdm KL25Z.


1 - Team Organisation and 6 - General Documents are not uploaded as they may contain non-relavant information that should not be made public


The team consisted of 5 members.

My personal contributions spanned all of the software, all of the mechanical design, a substantial part of the electronic hardware design, and most of the integration.
